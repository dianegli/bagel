Bagels are arguably an important food group here in the Big Apple. And while we all may have our favorite go-to spot, how accessible are bagels to you in NYC? To explore the bagel landscape in NYC, we scraped data from Yelp on all bagel shops and mapped out which NYC census tracts has the highest number of accessible bagel shops. Not surprisingly, there high variation in the number of accessible shops depending on where you live.
